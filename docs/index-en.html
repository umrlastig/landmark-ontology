Ontology Specification Draft

Description

Ontology of geographical objects used as reference objects in a mountain context.
This ontology is proposed during ANR Choucas project (ANR-16-CE23-0018).
It is inspired from GeOnto Ontology (Mustière et al., 2011) et LFRO ontology (Sinha, Gaurav et al. 2018):
  • Mustière Sébastien, Nathalie Abadie, Nathalie Aussenac- Gilles, Marie-Noelle Bessagnet, Mouna Kamel, et al.. Analyses linguistiques et techniques d alignement pour créer et enrichir une ontologie topographique. Revue Internationale de Géomatique, Lavoisier, 2011, 21 (2), pp.155-180
  • Sinha, Gaurav et al. 2018. ‘The Landform Reference Ontology (LFRO): A Foundation for Exploring Linguistic and Geospatial Conceptualization of Landforms (Short Paper)’. Schloss Dagstuhl - Leibniz-Zentrum fuer Informatik GmbH, Wadern/Saarbruecken, Germany.
